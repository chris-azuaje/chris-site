Profile

Welcome to my website! I'm Chris Azuaje, a graduate student with a passion for crafting functional and aesthetic applications. As a student I enjoy expanding my knowledge and exploring innovative ways to improve my technical skillset.

I originally pursued mechanical engineering as an undergrad which was an incredible and challenging journey. However, I came to realize that was not the path for me and eventually decided to transition into software.

My journey into software development began during a transformative year-long sabbatical exploring diverse cultures around the world. Witnessing the incredible impact of modern technologies in vibrant cities like Bangkok, juxtaposed with the absence of such advancements in places like Marrakesh, sparked my fascination with the potential of technology to drive positive change.

Today, I reside in the beautiful city of Charlotte where I spend most of my time studying code, working, creating projects, exercising, or enjoying the outdoors. Whatever I do, I approach every activity with a sense of purpose and a positive mindset!
