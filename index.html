Welcome to my website! I'm Chris, a software development student driven by a passion for crafting both functional and visually appealing applications. I enjoy expanding my knowledge and exploring innovative ways to improve my skillset.

My journey into software development began during a transformative year-long sabbatical exploring diverse cultures around the world. Witnessing the profound impact of modern technologies in bustling metropolises like Bangkok, juxtaposed with the absence of such advancements in places like Marrakesh, sparked my fascination with the potential of technology to drive positive change. Motivated by the opportunity to harness the power of IT to create meaningful solutions and improve lives globally, I embarked on this career path.

I currently reside in the beautiful city of Charlotte, North Carolina where I spend most of my time coding, exercising and enjoying the outdoors! Whatever I do, I approach every activity with a sense of purpose and a positive mindset.
