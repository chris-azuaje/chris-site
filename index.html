Profile

Welcome to my website! I'm Chris Azuaje, a UNC Charlotte student with a passion for crafting functional and visually appealing applications. As a student I enjoy expanding my knowledge and exploring innovative ways to improve my technical skillset.

I originally pursued mechanical engineering as an undergrad which was an incredible challenge. However, I came to realize that this was not the right direction for me and after some time decided to transition into software development.

My journey into software development began during a transformative year-long sabbatical exploring diverse cultures around the world. Witnessing the incredible impact of modern technologies in vibrant cities like Bangkok, juxtaposed with the absence of such advancements in places like Marrakesh, sparked my fascination with the potential of technology to drive positive change.

I currently reside in the beautiful city of Charlotte where I spend most of my studying code, working, creating projects, exercising, or enjoying the outdoors. Whatever I do, I approach every activity with a sense of purpose and a positive mindset!
